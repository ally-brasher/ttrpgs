Introduction to Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons is considered the main TTRPG. It is set to be a fanatsy adventure in a world differnet than our own. The game master is called a dungeon master.

This game is pretty rules heavy, as there are complicated spells and fighting mechanics. Depending on the group and dungeon master the rules can be changed to fit the group.

Important info

  1. Number of players:2-7
  2. Materials for players: six different types of dice: four sided, six sided, eight sided, ten sided, twelve sided, and twenty sided, charcter sheet, and notes as wanted
  3. Materials for the Dungeon Master: rule book, a full set of dide (as listed above), and notes

TTRPGs are for everyone, just find what you like and play!
